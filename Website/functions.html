What Can it Do?

There are many things you can ask the bot:

1) Can not find a building in campus. Just ask the bot.
- Where is chase ATM?
- Locate engineering building
- How to go to tower hall

2) Want to know about a professor?
- Tell me about Prof Rakesh Ranjan
- Office hours of Prof Dan Harkey?

3) Want to know real time weather in campus
- How is todays weather

4) Confusion about courses to take?
- Which are the subjects related to Data science
- Subjects in cloud computing

5) Want to find out if you can go to the library?
- Is library open now?
